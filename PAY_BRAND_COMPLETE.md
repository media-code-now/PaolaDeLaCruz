## Typography Usage

**Headings:** Cormorant Garamond (serif)
- Light weight for large titles
- Regular for subheadings
- Creates elegant, editorial feel

**Body Text:** Inter
- Clean, highly readable
- 400 weight for body
- 500-700 for emphasis

**Labels:** Inter
- Uppercase
- Wide letter-spacing (tracking-widest)
- Small size for sophistication
